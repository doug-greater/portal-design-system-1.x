Anatomy & rules

Trigger: the standard 44px floating-label field with a leading icon — calendar_today for single, date_range for range. Value formats as Jun 11, 2026 / Apr 20 – Apr 26, 2026 (en-dash range, per voice).

Popover: radius 10px, 1px --p-border, --shadow-float, anchored 8px below the field. Week starts Sunday. Weekday header is 500 11px --p-muted; day cells are 36px with a 32px circular hit target.

States: today = 1.5px inset --p-primary ring + primary text. Selected = solid --p-primary circle, white text. Adjacent-month days use --p-placeholder. Hover = --p-surface-tint.

Range & context-aware preset rails: endpoints are solid primary circles; the span between fills a --p-primary-tint band, rounded only at the ends. The left preset rail must match the field's temporal direction — forward (Today · Tomorrow · Next 7 / 30 Days · This Month · Next Month) for go-live dates, backward (Today · Yesterday · Last 7 / 14 / 30 Days) for audit / history. Rail fills --p-surface-alt; the active preset is a raised white row; a Clear (danger) pins to the bottom.

Bounds & triggers: min / fromDate disables earlier days (future-only fields enforce min = today); max disables later days (the Audit Log uses max = today). Besides the 44px field, a 36px filter-chip trigger (Date: from – to, tints when active) is used for table range filters.

Behavior: apply on second click for ranges (start → end); close on outside-click and Escape. The calendar is portaled to <body> and opens upward (dropUp) when there's no room below, so an in-modal picker never covers the Save button. Disabled days render --p-placeholder with no hover.
