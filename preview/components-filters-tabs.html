Two-pane popover: left rail lists every filterable attribute (active row = primary-tint fill, per-attribute count badge); right pane is the value selector. Enumerable attributes use a plain multi-select checkbox list. Large attributes (thousands of options) get a type-to-search field, a Select all N matches row that acts on the full match set, selected items pinned under SELECTED · N, and results capped at 50. A date range (type: "daterange", §1.4) is a first-class facet: the pane widens to 640px and embeds a range calendar; its value is {from, to}, it counts toward the "N filters" badge, and its token summarizes the range (Jun 10 – Jun 16). Retires one-off "Date" toolbar buttons. Filters apply live; Done only closes.
