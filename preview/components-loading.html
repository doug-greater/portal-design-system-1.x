Rules

Spinner: 2.5px ring, track --p-border, head --p-ink (near‑black in light, white in dark — matches the ink primary), 0.7s linear spin. Inside a filled button use the on-fill variant — the head inherits the button's foreground (currentColor), so it's --p-action-fg on the primary and white on danger, always contrasting the fill in both themes. Sizes 16 / 20 / 24.

Prefer skeletons over spinners for any layout whose shape is known — tables, stat rows, cards. They hold the page geometry so content doesn't jump. Reserve the bare spinner for unknown-shape or sub-second waits, and the inline spinner for in-place refreshes.

Skeleton: base #EDEFF2, a left-to-right white shimmer at 1.3s. Each block mirrors the real element's size & radius — text lines ~12–13px tall, pills 18px/999px, avatars circular. Render 3–6 placeholder rows, never a full page of them.

Motion: respect prefers-reduced-motion — drop the shimmer to a static tint and the spinner to a 1s step rotation.
