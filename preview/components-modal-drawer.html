Anatomy & rules

Overlay foundation: all three share one invisible primitive — scrim, focus-trap, Escape, return-focus, ARIA role="dialog". Modal and Drawer are the two visible surfaces built on it.

Modal · default: border-radius: 12px, 1px --p-border, shadow 0 8px 28px rgba(0,0,0,.18). Widths: sm 420 · md 520 · lg 640. Header (title + close), body, footer separated by 1px #F0F1F3. Footer right-aligned: Ghost/Neutral cancel + Primary confirm.

Modal · confirm: same shell + softer shadow. Adds a 34×34 icon chip (12% tint of the action tone), drops the close ✕, and uses a Ghost + tone-matched action footer. Optional #FFF7ED warning callout for downstream consequences — when present the action relabels to “Continue”.

Drawer shell: right-anchored, full content-height, border-left: 1px --p-border + --shadow-float. Widths: sm 400 · md 460 · lg 560. Header carries an overline + title; body scrolls; footer pinned with full-width actions. Use the drawer for view/edit-in-context without leaving the table; use the modal for short focused tasks.

Scrim: rgba(16,24,40,.45) over the page. No blur.

Motion: modal — 180ms ease-out, scale 0.98 → 1 + scrim fade. Drawer — 180ms ease-out translateX(16px → 0) (transform-only). Reduced-motion: no transform.

Behavior: trap focus; restore focus to the trigger on close. default & Drawer close on scrim-click, ✕, and Escape. confirm closes on Cancel or Escape only — never scrim-click — so a consequential choice can’t be dismissed by accident.
