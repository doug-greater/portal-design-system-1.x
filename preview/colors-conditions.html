Diverging, centered on green (Optimal = level 3). Reds/orange below, blues/violet above. A semantic palette — never re-skin per theme; a viewer must read "red = bad, green = good, blue/violet = too much."

level is the ordinal severity. Averaging a set of stores' levels and rounding to the nearest index yields a bin's representative condition — how the Coverage-Map hexbin reduces many SKUs/stores to one color.

Defined once in lib/conditions (JS, canonical) and mirrored as --cond-* tokens for CSS surfaces. Shared by the In-the-Market table's Condition cell, the coverage-panel legend, and the map hexbin + legend. The chip-tint sibling for At Risk is --g-orange-10 / --p-atrisk-strong (Chip atrisk tone).
