Ink‑forward color rule. Brand ink (--p-action) is the color of commitment & navigation — primary buttons, the active nav row, the active tab, the active wizard step, the active pagination page, and active view‑mode toggles. It inverts to a white surface in dark mode. Blue (--p-primary) is reserved for selection, state & focus inside content — links, focus rings, input/field focus, selected rows, applied filters, checkboxes/toggles, calendar selection, and info.
